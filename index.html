About Me


Hi, I'm Tim. I am a Senior Lecturer (equivalent to Associate Professor) in the Department of Informatics at King's College London. I am a UKRI Future Leaders Fellow

I focus on human-computer interaction and accessibility. My research considers two main areas: developing technologies that are more accessible to diverse populations with varying access needs, and creating technologies that support accessible communication. Specifically, I work in the domains of assistive technology, accessible digital content creation and consumption, and accessible healthcare technologies.

To learn more, take a look at my published work or the various bits of press coverage my work has attracted.

I also maintain a page on CHI statistics by year.
